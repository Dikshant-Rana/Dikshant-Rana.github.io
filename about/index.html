About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Orchid Beauty Parlour, where beauty meets expertise in the heart of Chisapani, Hetauda. For over 15 years, we have been dedicated to enhancing the natural beauty of our clients with professional care and attention to detail.

Our journey began with a simple passion for beauty and a commitment to making every client feel confident and radiant. What started as a small venture has grown into one of the most trusted beauty destinations in Hetauda, serving thousands of satisfied clients.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional beauty services that enhance your natural beauty while ensuring you feel comfortable, valued, and absolutely beautiful.
