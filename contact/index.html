Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting and ensure your preferred time slot.

What products do you use?

We use only premium quality, branded beauty products that are safe for all skin types and provide the best results.

Do you offer bridal packages?

Yes, we specialize in bridal makeup and styling. Our bridal packages include pre-wedding consultations, trial sessions, and the complete wedding day makeover.
