Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for enrolling?

Anyone interested in learning beauty services can enroll. No prior experience is required for basic courses. Minimum age requirement is 16 years.

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

Yes, we provide certificates for all courses upon successful completion and passing the practical examination.

What are the batch timings?

We offer flexible timings with morning batches (9 AM - 12 PM) and afternoon batches (1 PM - 4 PM). Weekend batches are also available upon request.

How many students are there in each batch?

We maintain small batch sizes of 6-8 students to ensure personalized attention and adequate practice opportunities for each student.

Will I get job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide guidance for starting your own business and help connect students with job opportunities in beauty parlours across Hetauda and surrounding areas.

Are course materials and products included?

All practice materials and products are provided during training. Students will receive a course kit with essential materials at the beginning.
